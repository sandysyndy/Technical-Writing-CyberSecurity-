### 🛠️ Continuous Learning: Technical Writing Foundations (Week 1)

This week, I focused on unlearning academic writing habits and mastering the core mechanics of clear, predictable technical documentation. My tracking modules covered:

*   **Active Voice Architecture:** Stripping out passive structures that hide accountability in high-stakes environments (e.g., transforming *"the alert was triggered"* into *"the system triggers the alert"*).
*   **Terminology Consistency:** Eliminating synonyms and vocabulary drifting to ensure critical technical terms remain 100% predictable across workflows.
*   **Acronym Management:** Implementing the industry standard "Rule of First Use" to properly introduce complex technical terms without breaking reader momentum.
*   **Information Design:** Using structured list logic, clear headers, and plain-text Markdown formatting to build highly scannable layouts.
